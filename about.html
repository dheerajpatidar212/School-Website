SCHOOL OVERVIEWS

St. Paul's Convent school is an English Medium Co-educational Institution run by St. Paul's Convent Samiti, a registered body. Also, with a unique affiliation for Hindi Medium like Jawahar Navodaya & Central school. SPCS is senior secondary school, affiliated to CBSE The SPCS was established in June, 1990. Since it's inception the school is continuously setting up benchmark in the field of education as well as in other activities.

SPC is located at Jaora in Ratlam District of M.P., 34 km away from district place. The campus is situated in completely lush green area and pollution free natural surrounding. As per CBSE guidelines, today's education should be child-centered, therefore, SPCS is providing such a constructive environment which is indispensable for overall development of children.

Today, the world has become a global village. Hence, globalization of culture, commerce and trade politics has become a reality. Therefore schools must provide infrastructure and environment not merely for information and knowledge, but for the Education of the children so as to enable them to face the ramifications of globalization. SPCS, being thoroughly aware of it, is providing an environment where children acquire global perspective through introduction to various cultures, civilization and religion of the world.In our school, children are encouraged to give reign to their fancy and imagination without any restriction for further exploration. The mere desire and inclination to think new thoughts, to invent and to discover are adding a completely new dimension to the process of learning.We have got inhouse training programmes for teachers and also sending teachers to outside training courses organized by both CBSE and other organizations to incorporate skills, competencies and values with knowledge.
