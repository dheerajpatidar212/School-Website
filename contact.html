About Saint Paul's Convent School

St. Paul's Convent school is an English Medium Co-educational Institution run by St. Paul's Convent Samiti, a registered body. Also, with a unique affiliation for Hindi Medium like Jawahar Navodaya & Central school. SPCS is senior secondary school, affiliated to CBSE The SPCS was established in June, 1990. Since it's inception the school is continuously setting up benchmark in the field of education as well as in other activities.
